WALL-E

Description

A cute picture of a fictional trash collecting robot. When thinking of a character to use, somehow this was the first thing that appeared. A character from a very good movie that I cried over when I was a kid. The story of WALL-E holds very important themes of sustainability and physical health, and he is adorable.

  • He is a cute robot, and honestly it was fun to do this project while remembering how much I love it as a kid
  • Reminds me how important nature conservation is and also how important physical health is
  • When he was getting crushed by the platform, I was crying
